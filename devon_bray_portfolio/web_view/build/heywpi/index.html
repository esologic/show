@heywpi (January of 2015)

Twitter-enabled dorm window.

A strand of LEDs hung in my street-facing college dorm room could color based on input from a public twitter account. Accepted text or images as input. After the color change was completed, an image of the result was replied back to user. This concept eventually grew to become Telapush. A preeminent experience of mine in the thrilling medium of public installation art.
