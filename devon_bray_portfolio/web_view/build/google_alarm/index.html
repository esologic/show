Raspberry Pi Google Calendar Alarm Clock (June of 2013)

Uses Google calendar to trigger music playback on a set of speakers via a Raspberry Pi.

An ancient project, one of the very first published on the blog. Used the freshly released Raspberry Pi to prevent my high school self from sleeping in. This post was featured on lifehacker.com, a major technology publication. The thrilling rush of comments, feedback that resulted from this post is probably what lead to me continuing to publish my work in this way for so long. Little did I know at the time, but both the Raspberry Pi and Python would remain a major part of my life for years to come. It's probably inappropriate to include such an old project in a portfolio like this but this work will always have a place in my heart, and is the earliest example of my affinity for mixing disparate inputs and outputs.
