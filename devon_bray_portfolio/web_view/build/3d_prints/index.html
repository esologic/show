3D Print Gallery (October of 2020)

Over the years I've published a number of standalone 3D printable designs. This entry aggregates the most useful and most interesting.

3D Printing is a favorite technology and it is leveraged heavily in my work, professional and otherwise. For mechanical CAD, my go-to software suite is Solidworks but occasionally I've utilized Fusion 360 or ZBrush. In my studio I use a Prusa i3 Mk2s, but have worked intimately with Formlabs, Stratasys and LulzBot machines as well. Typically my designs are utilitarian in nature.
