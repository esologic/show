Multiple Audio (February of 2019)

n-Channel Audio Driver for Sound Sculpture.

Client needed a way to have looping audio play sequentially/simultaneously on 8 different speakers housed in a sculpture. Had the additional requirement that audio needed to be changed over time and couldn't be "hardcoded", would need to be replaced by a non-technical gallery attendant. Total turnaround time for this project was under three weeks, and is one of a series of jobs I've completed for interdisciplinary sculpture artist, Sara Dittrich.
