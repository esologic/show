GANce (November of 2021)

Tool to map music and video into the latent space of StyleGAN2 networks.

In collaboration with Won Pound for his forthcoming album release via minaret records I was recently commissioned to lead an expedition into latent space, encountering intelligences of my own haphazard creation. Created the camera used to capture training images, then trained StyleGAN2 networks. Experimentation lead to new methods to input music and video into the networks. Note that this tool is an active work in progress.

Introductory blog post. Contains more information about technique, as well as examples with sound.
