Block The Wind Festival 2019 (August of 2019)

13 artist independent music festival.

Fifth annual music festival. Back at One Longfellow Square in Portland, Maine. Featured performances by musicians and artist vendor booths. Musicians: GOD.DAMN.CHAN, B. Aull, Won Pound, Shane Reis, EMRLD, Bensbeendead., Jxck, Just Plain Jones, Just Milk, The Ravine, Angelikah Fahray. Featured artists: Jacob A. M. Powers, Emily Cole. Full event credits here. Pandemic has since put a damper on these festivals but we plan to come back when the time is right.

See summary post on www.blockthewindfestival.com for more photos.
